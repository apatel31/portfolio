General Motors Logic Analysis

Through utilizing external data analysis technology, we can quantify categorical data. Bump Chart's show how rankings of dimensions change over time in relation to one another. Yoshikoder analyzes different logical themes from a collection of texts. This visualization shows how logical themes have changed in relation to one another on the basis of General Motor's 10K's since 2014

This analysis can be done with many other organizations, but in the case of General Motors, Civic and Market logic themes in their 10K's continue to reign amongst the rest. However, we can see how Popular logic themes are growing as a presence in GM's 10Ks in relation to other logics.

The below dashboard shows the distribution of logic themes across various documentation published by General Motors, including their glassdoor page and sustainability report.
