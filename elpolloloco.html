El Pollo Loco Regression

This dataset shows a regression analysis on El Pollo Loco restaurant ratings based on the percentage of hispanic population in California. Regression analysis tools enable us to quantify and predict trends in data over time. A positive linear correlation shows a strong ability to predict future datapoints

In the case of this regression analysis, there is a negative linear correlation between restaurant ratings and hispanic populations. However, our r-squared value is not significant enough to draw and casual conclusions about the data.
