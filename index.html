Data Visualization Portfolio Menu

Hi! My name is Ankit Patel and this portfolio consists of a series of embedded and interactive Tableau dashboards that I have created over the span of my work visualizing data. I was exposed to the software through high school robotics and have spent many hours developing various dashboards with diverse datasets.

Although much of my work is protected with private data, there are many personal projects I have worked on myself with open source information. These vary from Airbnb's in New York City to Fantasy Football insights for drafting the best fantasy football team. Each page goes into greater detail of these projects and they each have an embedded dashboards that you can interact with and modify the visualization.

Below is a gallery of screenshots for some visualizations that I really enjoy making in Tableau. These range from simple bar charts or line graphs that get the job done to more advanced visuals like bump charts or geocoded heat maps.

Thank you for taking time out to look at some of my work, I hope you enjoy it!
